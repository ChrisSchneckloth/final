Resume Builder

This site is a forum that you fill out, and once you do, it will generate a basic resume for you. The form will not create though until an email is put into the forms.

Shopping List

This site is a basic grocery list that you can create. You type in an item you want, and it will appear on your list. As you are shopping, you can cross off, or really, delete, the first or last item of the list if you would like.
